Aliko Dangote

The Nigerian businessman and industrialist.He is best known as the founder, chairman, and CEO of the Dangote Group,the largest industrial conglomerate in West Africa.

Here is a Timeline of Aliko Dangote's life

  • 1957 - Dangote was born into a wealthy Hausa Muslim family on 10 April 1957 in Kano
  • 1978 - In 1978, he graduated from the Government College, Birnin Kudu
  • 1977 - The Dangote Group was established as a small trading firm in 1977, the same year Dangote relocated to Lagos to expand the company.
  • 1990 - he approached the Central Bank of Nigeria with the idea that it would be less expensive for the bank to allow his transport company to manage their fleet of staff buses, a proposal that was also approved.
  • 2007 - Dangote became Nigeria's first billionaire in 2007.
  • 2011 - Dangote was appointed by President Goodluck Jonathan to serve as a member of his economic management team.
  • 2012 - Dangote approached the Nigerian Ports Authority to lease an abandoned piece of land at the Apapa Port, which was approved.He later built facilities for his sugar company there.
